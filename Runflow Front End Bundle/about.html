About RunFlow

RunFlow is a tool to support Fredericton Marathon operations. It provides insights into:

  • Level of Service (LOS): How comfortable and safe the course feels for participants.
  • Density: How many runners occupy a course section at a given time.
  • Flow Rate: The number of runners passing through a section per minute.

These measures help race directors, course marshals, and health & safety leaders make operational decisions before and during race weekend.
